Q3 :Why is bias mitigation critical when using AI for user experience personalization?
Suggested Answer

>Bias mitigation is important because personalization algorithms may:

>Favor certain user groups over others.

>Show unfair recommendations.

>Exclude or misrepresent minority users.

If bias isn't addressed, the AI can produce unfair user experiences, reduce trust, and create ethical or legal issues. Mitigation tools help ensure equal and inclusive personalization.
